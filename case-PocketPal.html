Overview

A compact solution to simplify your daily life

The PocketPal is a revolutionary product designed to keep all your essentials in one convenient, accessible place. By attaching securely to the back of your phone, it ensures that your cards, keys, and cash are always within reach. Gone are the days of scrambling to find your wallet or fumbling through your bag at the last minute. The PocketPal is sleek, lightweight, and thoughtfully designed to fit seamlessly into your daily routine. Whether you're heading to class, the gym, or a night out, PocketPal has you covered.

Concept

Inspired by real-world challenges

The concept for PocketPal was born from a simple question: "What daily challenges do people face when organizing their essentials?" After conducting interviews with college freshmen, we identified a common pain point—carrying multiple small items like keys, cards, and cash without losing or misplacing them. We set out to create a product that would solve this issue while maintaining a balance of functionality and style. PocketPal combines the essential elements of a wallet, keyholder, and phone attachment into a single product, offering a streamlined solution for modern users.

Its design is not just practical but also inclusive, addressing issues like the lack of adequate storage in women’s clothing and the need for compact solutions in everyday life. PocketPal bridges the gap between convenience and innovation, making it a product for everyone.

Design Process

From ideation to final product

The journey to creating PocketPal involved extensive research, brainstorming, and iterative development. We started with sketches and prototypes, gathering feedback to refine the design at every stage. Our team experimented with various materials, attachment methods, and storage configurations to ensure that the final product would be durable, efficient, and easy to use.

Key milestones in the design process included:

  • Conducting user interviews to understand their needs and preferences.
  • Creating CAD models and running simulations to optimize dimensions and functionality.
  • Prototyping the hinge mechanism for quick and seamless access to stored items.
  • Testing the adhesive strength and durability under various conditions to ensure reliability.
The result is a thoughtfully engineered product that seamlessly integrates into everyday life.

Specifications

Key details and features
  • Capacity: Holds up to 3 cards, 1 key, and 5 bills
  • Material: ABS plastic for durability and lightweight
  • Attachment: 3M adhesive for secure and easy removal
  • Size: 5 cm x 10 cm x 0.5 cm
  • Weight: Less than 50g
  • Customization: Multiple color and design options

Result

Impact and market potential

The PocketPal has been a game-changer in the personal accessory space. By offering a unique combination of convenience, style, and utility, it appeals to a wide audience, from college students to busy professionals. Early feedback from prototypes showed high satisfaction rates, with users praising its functionality and compact design. PocketPal not only simplifies the organization of daily essentials but also reduces the stress of misplacing items, saving time and money.

With a low manufacturing cost and high perceived value, the PocketPal holds immense potential for market success. The product fills a gap in the market for multi-functional phone accessories and has the versatility to adapt to evolving consumer needs. PocketPal is poised to redefine how people carry their essentials, one phone at a time.
